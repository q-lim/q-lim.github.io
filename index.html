About


Quentin Lim graduated from the National University of Singapore in 2023 with Honours (Distinction) in Materials Science & Engineering in the Research-Focused Pathway with a specialisation in Polymeric and Biomedical Materials, and a Minor in Forensic Science. Quentin has a strong passion for research, and a background in biomaterials, analytical chemistry, biochemistry, polymer chemistry, materials science and engineering.

Quentin is a compassionate individual who gets a sense of fulfilment and contentment knowing he can contribute to the area of research and development and play a role in enhancing the health of many people. He is a highly self-motivated, results-driven, and disciplined team player who has a collaborative mindset, works well with others, under minimal supervision. He is also a fast learner and a proactive problem solver who takes the initiative to propose new solutions to any existing problems encountered.

During his studies, Quentin was a Varsity Athlete in the National University of Singapore Dragonboat Team, representing the University in both the Mens' and Mixed racing categories. He is a leader by example and embodied the team's values of, Camaraderie, Fire and Brotherhood, displaying excellent sportsmanship in his behaviour and conduct. He was a selfless team player who always gave 100% for the team, constantly pushing the team to strive and work hard to become better. Quentin's effort and leadership was vital to the Dragonboat Team clinching multiple medals between 2019 to 2023, which included Quentin himself winning back-to-back Gold Medals in the Mixed racing category.

Quentin is a self-motivated, highly disciplined, logical individual who is a systemic problem solver and has a strong passion to learn. Quentin aspires to make a positive impact on people's lives with science and engineering.
